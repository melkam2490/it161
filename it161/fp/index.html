Ethiopian traditional clothing is rich in history and cultural significance. The traditional attire of Ethiopia is known as the "habesha kemis" for women and "shamma" for men. The story behind these garments reflects the country's diverse heritage and longstanding traditions. The habesha kemis is a long, white cotton dress typically made from handwoven fabric. It features intricate embroidery and decorative patterns along the them, neckline, and sleeves. The designs often incorporate vibrant colors such as red, blue, and gold, reflecting the Ethiopian love for bright and bold hues.
